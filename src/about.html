Existing adaptive management and resource partitioning strategies developed for resilient infrastructures are often static, based on rules developed by experts, and dependent on centralized control.

The SWARM project explores how Swarm Intelligence (SI) can provide robust, performant, resilient, and fault-tolerant execution of DOE scientific workflows.

Research Objective

"The goal is to design SI-based resilient IRI that can quickly recover from failures, adapt to changes in the environment, and maximize resource utilization."
